Course Overview

Welcome to the "Introduction to Artificial Intelligence" section. In this course, you will embark on a journey that will introduce you to one of the most rapidly growing and fascinating fields in the world today.

As you explore this section, you will learn about the following topics:

  • Introduction to AI: Gain insights into the world of Artificial Intelligence and its potential impact.
  • Definition of AI: Understand the core definitions and principles that underpin AI.
  • Related Fields: Explore the different fields related to AI, such as Machine Learning, Neural Networks, and more.
  • The Turing Test: Learn about this iconic test that is used to measure a machine's ability to exhibit intelligent behavior.

We hope this course will provide you with a strong foundation and inspire you to delve deeper into the fascinating world of AI. Enjoy your journey!
