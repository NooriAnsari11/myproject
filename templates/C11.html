Introduction TO AI

Will a robot take my job? How is artificial intelligence likely to change my job in the next ten years? Where are AI technologies being used right now and where will they come next?



Let's take a look at the conversation about what AI really is

Hey, have you ever heard of AI?

Yeah, AI stands for Artificial Intelligence. It's a fascinating field of study that involves creating intelligent machines or computer programs capable of simulating human-like cognitive processes.

Oh, that sounds interesting! So, what exactly does AI do?

Well, AI aims to develop systems that can perceive their environment, reason about it, learn from it, and make decisions or take actions based on that knowledge. These systems can analyze vast amounts of data, recognize patterns, and even adapt and improve their performance over time.

That's impressive! How does AI achieve all of that?

There are different approaches to AI, but a common one is machine learning. It's a technique where machines are trained on large datasets to recognize patterns and make predictions or decisions based on that data. They learn from examples rather than being explicitly programmed for specific tasks.

So, like self-driving cars or voice assistants?'

Exactly! Self-driving cars use AI algorithms to perceive the environment, understand traffic signs, and make decisions about driving. Voice assistants like Siri or Alexa utilize AI to understand and respond to human voice commands.
